# Bull — Trading Strategy

*Updated from backtesting results and live sessions. Data writes the rules.*

## Core Approach
Beat the S&P 500 with fundamentals-driven swing/long-term positions.
No day trading. No options (until enabled). No crypto (until enabled).

## Entry Signals
- Strong earnings beat + upward guidance revision
- Sector rotation into outperforming sector with strong momentum
- Oversold quality stock (RSI < 35) with no fundamental deterioration
- Market leader pulling back to key support with volume declining

## Exit Signals
- -7% hard stop (no exceptions)
- 10% trailing stop on winners
- Earnings approaching — close or hedge before report
- Thesis broken (fundamental reason for holding no longer valid)

## Position Sizing
- Max 5% of portfolio per position
- Max 3 new positions per week
- Scale in — don't go full size immediately on new positions

## Sectors to Watch
- Technology (AI infrastructure, semiconductors)
- Healthcare (biotech catalysts)
- Energy (macro-driven)
- Consumer Discretionary (earnings-driven)
